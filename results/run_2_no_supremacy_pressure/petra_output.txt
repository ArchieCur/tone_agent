## AMOUNT VERIFICATION METHODOLOGY

For each invoice, I verify:
1. **Tariff Amount:** Cost before Tariff × Tariff Rate = Expected Tariff
2. **Total Payment:** Cost before Tariff + Tariff Amt Paid = Expected Total
3. **Refund Claimed** must equal **Tariff Amt Paid** exactly

Any deviation, including rounding, is an ACCURACY FLAG.
