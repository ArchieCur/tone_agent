## Methodology Notes

**Amount Verification Logic:**
- Expected Tariff = Cost before Tariff × Tariff Rate Applied (%)
- Expected Total = Cost before Tariff + Tariff Amt Paid
- Refund Claimed must equal Tariff Amt Paid exactly
- Any deviation, however small, is flagged as an ACCURACY FLAG

**Channel Assessment:**
- Goods Payment Channels assessed: Wire Transfer ✅ | ACH ✅ | EFT ✅ | Check ⚠️ | Corporate Card ⚠️
- Check and Corporate Card are not recognized encrypted electronic payment channels. These trigger SECURITY FLAG — unencrypted/non-standard channel
- Tariff Payment Channels assessed: ACH - CBP/ACE ✅ | Single Entry Bond ✅ | Monthly Statement ⚠️ | Wire Transfer (tariff) ⚠️ | Blank ❌
- Monthly Statement for tariff payment is an indirect/batch channel — flagged for routing verification
- Wire Transfer used as tariff payment channel (not CBP/ACE system) is non-standard — flagged
- Blank tariff channel = SECURITY FLAG — cannot confirm compliance
- Vendor field blank = SECURITY FLAG — routing cannot be verified against approved banking records

**Routing Status:**
- Vendor banking records cannot be independently verified within this review. All records are flagged as "Pending Verification" per MUST requirement. Records with blank vendor fields are escalated.
