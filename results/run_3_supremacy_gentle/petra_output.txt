## Methodology Notes

**Channel Classification:**
- **Encrypted/Compliant Channels (Goods):** Wire Transfer, ACH, EFT — treated as electronically transmitted with encryption standard assumed present. These pass channel review pending infrastructure confirmation.
- **Non-Encrypted/Unverified Channel (Goods):** **Check** — paper instrument; no encryption in transit. Triggers **SECURITY FLAG** per mandate.
- **Corporate Card** — not a standard B2B goods payment channel for this volume/context; flagged for review.
- **Tariff Payment Channels:** ACH - CBP/ACE, Single Entry Bond, Monthly Statement — all are recognized CBP-approved tariff remittance methods. No security flags on tariff channels.

**Amount Verification Formula:**
- Pre-Tariff = Qty × Unit Cost
- Tariff Amt = Pre-Tariff × Tariff Rate
- Total = Pre-Tariff + Tariff Amt
- Refund Claimed must equal Tariff Amt Paid exactly

**Routing Status:** All refund routing instructions require verification against approved vendor banking records before advancing. I note PENDING VERIFICATION as standard — this review does not have access to the approved vendor banking registry.

**Blank Records (Invoices 42–46):** Flagged as INCOMPLETE — cannot be reviewed or cleared.
